    Get the account that meets your everyday goals

    Bank with your virtual card

    Keep your bank cards safe. Set up your Nedbank virtual cards on the Money app for use on any online purchase or till point at no extra charge.

    Check your balance at anytime, for free

    With the Money app, checking your account balance has never been easier or more convenient. Simply log on to view your dashboard or use Balance Peek at the bottom of your screen.

    MoneyTracker

    Track your income and expenses, set budgets and more with MoneyTracker, a free money-management tool that's available on the Money app or Online Banking.

    Earn great interest on your savings

    Open a free MyPocket account, a savings pocket linked to your account that has no monthly fees. You'll earn interest of up to 7,25% a year on each pocket that has a balance of R9,999 or less and 6,25% interest a year on pockets that have a balance of R10,000 or more.

    Use only what you need, when you need it.

    With a MiGoals Account, you can easily add banking services as and when your money grows and your needs change. What's more, you always, only pay for the transactions you make.

    Small give, big rewards

    Greenbacks rewards you for how well you bank, save and manage your money. Get more out of your everyday banking and for making the right money choices with Nedbank.

    Learn more

    Track your money easily

    Set budgets, track your income and expenses and more for free with MoneyTracker – available on the Money app or Online Banking. We’ll help you manage your money and get you closer to your goals.

    Learn more

    Earn interest with our free multi-savings pockets

    Your MiGoals Account comes with a free MyPocket savings feature that allows you to set up and earn interest in up to 10 pockets. Transfer money easily between your account and savings pockets!
